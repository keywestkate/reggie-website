Expert Advice

📋 Reggie's Island Ready
Moving Checklist

Prepare your property before we arrive and the job goes faster — saving you time and money.

1 Disconnect all appliances (fridge, washer, dryer)
2 Label all boxes clearly by room
3 Schedule utility transfers before move day
4 Clear all pathways, stairs & parking areas
5 Confirm property address and gate/key access
6 Keep an essentials box accessible (don't pack it)
7 Notify neighbors if parking space is needed
8 Have payment method ready for day-of balance

⏱️ Remember the Wait-Time Policy: If our crew arrives and must wait more than 30 minutes before we can start — due to access issues, unpacked items, or other delays — wait time is billed at 1 hour of labor. Being prepared protects your budget and keeps the island moving!

Can you handle our whole move — pack, haul, clean, and landscape? +
Yes — that's exactly what we're built for. R&S is Key West's only full-service property crew. We move you, deep-clean your old space, haul every piece of junk, and landscape your new property. One call, one team, one invoice.
What areas do you serve? +
We serve Key West, Stock Island, Big Coppitt Key, and surrounding Monroe County. Not sure if we cover your area? Call Reggie at (305) 589-8981 and we'll confirm.
What are your hours? +
Monday through Sunday, 7:00 AM to 7:00 PM. Reggie answers calls and texts 7 days a week.
How does the wait-time policy work? +
If our crew arrives on time and must wait more than 30 minutes before the job can start — due to access issues, unpacked items, or other delays — that wait time is billed as 1 hour of labor. We do this to be fair to our crew and to other clients scheduled that day. Being prepared (see checklist above) keeps everything on track.
