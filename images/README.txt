📸 PHOTO SLOTS — Add Reggie's photos here

Drop these files into this folder (images/) to make them appear on the site.
Use JPG format. Rename the files exactly as shown below.

REQUIRED PHOTOS:
----------------

1. hero.jpg
   — The big background photo behind "Clean Spaces, Clear Minds"
   — Best choice: wide landscape shot of a freshly detailed car, clean boat,
     pressure-washed driveway, or a job in progress
   — Minimum size: 1400px wide
   — After adding, open index.html and find the hero__bg-img img tag,
     change opacity from 0 to 0.45

2. reggie-portrait.jpg
   — Photo of Reggie himself in the About section
   — Best choice: Reggie in uniform, smiling, in front of a clean vehicle or workspace
   — Portrait orientation (taller than wide)
   — After adding, replace the div.about__photo-placeholder with:
     <img src="images/reggie-portrait.jpg" alt="Reggie, owner of R&S Cleaning Solutions"
          class="about__photo" width="480" height="600" loading="lazy" />

3. og-image.jpg
   — Used when people share the site on Facebook, iMessage, etc.
   — Best choice: logo + business name on a clean background, or a great before/after
   — Size: 1200 x 630px
